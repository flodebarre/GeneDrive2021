Gene Drive Workshop

21-24 June, 2021

 

This workshop on synthetic gene drives aims to gather researchers, risk assessors and regulators from various backgrounds to discuss interdisplinary aspects of gene drives. It will feature various sessions: molecular biology, theoretical models, risk assessment, ethical issues, governance and public engagement.

The workshop will take place online on June 21-24, 2021. To accommodate for the different time zones of the participants, we plan to have two sessions per day, at 14:00-16:00 (UTC) and then 17:00-20:00 (UTC).
